Making Characters for my game: Part I

Recently, I have been thinking a lot about Pokémon, and more specifically about how I would change it if I were the designer. The actual changes are story for a different blogpost, but suffice it to say that, within a few days, I was planning my own game in the same style.

As I was thinking about story elements, I realised I would need to create character designs. The main characters for Pokémon games usually have really cool designs, and I want to create something similar, but with characters that are a bit older.

In the previous article, I showed you guys my journey trying to create a 3d model. I decided that I would start over with a model that I could actually use.

At first, I thought I'd try using the Automatic Quad Remesher to automate the retopology. However, I found that the mesh it generated didn't play well with rigging.

I thus decided to manually retopologize the whole model. And you know what? It wasn't that bad! Now that I wasn't obsessively following the video tutorial, I was much faster and more motivated. Instead of copying Bran Sculpts's every move, I was coming up with my own solutions to the problems I faced. Now that I understood the purpose of retopology, I was able to create a much better flow.

But when I went back to rigging, the problems returned. Now, the rig was failing to generate because I deleted the wrong bones or something. I then re-created the rig from scratch, but then different problems showed up. Several body parts were moving in ways they weren't supposed to. Basically every body part would warp strangely when it moved (often in the wrong direction), and took other body parts with it.

Unfortunately, I have yet to resolve this problem. When I have time, I will update y'all again.

In the meantime, I also took up drawing again. I decided I would start from the basics, just sort of copying reference images. Unfortunately, all of the characters I drew are copyrighted, so I can't show you the results, but I think they're pretty good.

What i can show you are a few terrible sketches I made of some of the characters. Once the model is finished, I hope to use it as a basis for these designs.
